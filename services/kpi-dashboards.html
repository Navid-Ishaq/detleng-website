Our KPI Dashboard Development Process

  1. Consultation
    We begin by understanding your business objectives, reporting priorities, and the KPIs that matter most to your organization.
  2. Requirement Analysis
    DeTLeng reviews your existing reports, data sources, and business processes to define the most valuable dashboard solution.
  3. Planning
    A dashboard strategy is created, including KPI selection, layout design, data integration, and reporting requirements.
  4. Development
    Our team builds interactive dashboards, develops visual reports, integrates data sources, and creates meaningful KPI calculations.
  5. Testing

  6. Delivery
    The completed KPI Dashboard is deployed with documentation and guidance to help your team use it effectively.
  7. Ongoing Support
    DeTLeng continues to optimize dashboards, refine KPIs, and support your evolving business reporting needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a KPI Dashboard?

A KPI Dashboard is an interactive reporting tool that displays your most important business metrics in a clear and visual format, helping you monitor performance and make informed decisions.

Why are KPI Dashboards important?

KPI Dashboards provide real-time visibility into business performance, allowing decision-makers to identify trends, monitor progress, and respond quickly to changing conditions.

Can DeTLeng create dashboards for different departments?

Yes. DeTLeng develops customized KPI Dashboards for executives, finance, sales, marketing, operations, customer service, and other business functions.

Can KPI Dashboards update automatically?

Absolutely. We integrate dashboards with your business systems so KPI data refreshes automatically without manual updates.

Which platforms can DeTLeng use?

Depending on your requirements, DeTLeng develops KPI Dashboards using Power BI, Looker Studio, and other Business Intelligence platforms.

Can existing reports be converted into KPI Dashboards?

Yes. DeTLeng can modernize traditional spreadsheet-based reporting by transforming it into interactive KPI Dashboards that are easier to understand and maintain.
