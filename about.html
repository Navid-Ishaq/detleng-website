About DeTLeng

Data Engineering & Analytics Solutions that help organizations transform raw data into meaningful business outcomes.

Who We Are

DeTLeng is a Data Engineering and Analytics consultancy focused on helping organizations improve reporting, streamline data processes, and make better business decisions.

We work with businesses that need reliable data solutions, automated reporting, modern analytics platforms, and actionable insights that support growth and operational efficiency.

Our work combines Data Engineering, Analytics, Cloud Technologies, Business Intelligence, and Reporting Automation to deliver practical solutions that create measurable value.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

Transform complex data into clear business value.

We help organizations move beyond spreadsheets, manual reporting, and disconnected systems by building modern data solutions that improve visibility, accuracy, and decision-making.

Every solution we deliver is designed to help businesses save time, reduce reporting effort, and gain greater confidence in their data.

Our Approach

At DeTLeng, every project starts with understanding the business challenge behind the data.

Rather than focusing only on technology, we focus on delivering solutions that support business objectives and produce measurable results.

Our approach is built around:

  • Understanding Business Requirements
  • Building Reliable Data Foundations
  • Automating Reporting Processes
  • Designing Scalable Solutions
  • Delivering Clear Documentation
  • Creating Long-Term Business Value

Whether the solution involves ETL pipelines, Power BI dashboards, BigQuery analytics, or reporting automation, our goal remains the same: helping organizations make better decisions through better data.

Our Story

DeTLeng was created with a simple belief: businesses should spend less time managing data and more time using it.

Many organizations collect large amounts of information but struggle to transform that data into useful insights. Reporting becomes time-consuming, processes become fragmented, and decision-making becomes more difficult.

DeTLeng was established to bridge that gap by providing practical Data Engineering and Analytics solutions that help organizations organize, understand, and leverage their data more effectively.

Today, DeTLeng continues to support businesses through consulting services, analytics solutions, reporting automation, and real-world data projects designed to deliver measurable business outcomes.
