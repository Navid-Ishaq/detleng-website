About DeTLeng

Data Engineering, ETL & Analytics Engineering Solutions

DeTLeng helps organizations transform raw, disconnected, and inconsistent data into analytics-ready business assets that support reporting, business intelligence, and better decision-making.

We believe that dashboards, reports, and KPIs are only as reliable as the data behind them.

That is why our primary focus is not visualization.

Our focus is building the data foundation that makes visualization trustworthy.

Who We Are

DeTLeng is a practical Data Engineering, ETL, and Analytics Engineering consultancy focused on helping organizations structure, validate, transform, and prepare data for analytics, reporting, and decision-making.

We work with businesses that need reliable data foundations, modern analytics environments, automated workflows, centralized reporting datasets, and scalable solutions capable of supporting future growth.

Our work combines Data Engineering, ETL Development, SQL Analytics, Data Warehousing, Automation, Cloud Technologies, and Business Intelligence practices to create practical solutions that deliver measurable business value.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

Transform Complex Data Into Clear Business Value.

We help organizations move beyond spreadsheets, manual reporting processes, disconnected systems, and inconsistent datasets by building structured, reliable, and analytics-ready data environments.

Every solution is designed to improve visibility, reduce reporting effort, increase confidence in data, and support better business decisions.

Our Philosophy

Most organizations do not have dashboard problems.

They have data problems.

Data is often fragmented across spreadsheets, files, cloud applications, operational systems, databases, ERP platforms, and CRM environments.

Before meaningful analytics can occur, that data must be collected, validated, standardized, transformed, and structured.

The DeTLeng Principle

We Don't Just Build Dashboards.

We Engineer the Data That Makes Dashboards Trustworthy.

This philosophy influences every project we undertake.

Beyond Technology

At DeTLeng, technology is never the starting point.

Business challenges come first.

Rather than promoting specific tools, we focus on understanding business requirements, identifying opportunities for improvement, simplifying complexity, and delivering practical solutions that solve real-world problems.

Technology is selected only after the business need is clearly understood.

This ensures solutions remain practical, maintainable, scalable, and aligned with organizational objectives.

Our Story

DeTLeng was created after recognizing a common challenge faced by many organizations.

Businesses collect increasing amounts of data, yet many continue to struggle with fragmented reporting, inconsistent metrics, manual processes, and limited visibility into performance.

The challenge is rarely the absence of data.

The challenge is transforming that data into a trusted business asset.

DeTLeng was established to bridge that gap by helping organizations organize, engineer, validate, and prepare data for analytics, reporting, and decision-making.

Today, DeTLeng continues to build practical solutions that help businesses improve visibility, automate processes, reduce reporting complexity, and create measurable value through better use of data.

Reporting & Business Intelligence Support

Reporting tools remain an important part of the analytics ecosystem.

When required, DeTLeng supports reporting and visualization platforms including Power BI, Looker Studio, Excel, Tableau, and other business intelligence solutions.

However, reporting is considered the final layer built on top of a well-engineered data foundation.

Our priority is ensuring the underlying data is accurate, reliable, and analytics-ready before visualization begins.
