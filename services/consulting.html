  1. Initial Consultation

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of consulting does DeTLeng provide?

DeTLeng offers consulting for Data Engineering, Business Intelligence, Reporting Automation, Analytics Engineering, Data Warehousing, BigQuery, AI solutions, and business data strategy.

Do I need a technical background?

Not at all. We explain every recommendation in clear, simple language so both technical and non-technical stakeholders can make informed decisions.

Can DeTLeng review our existing reporting environment?

Yes. We can assess your current reporting processes, identify challenges, and recommend practical improvements.

Do I have to purchase implementation services?

No. Consulting is valuable on its own. If you decide to move forward, DeTLeng can also help implement the recommended solutions.

Can consulting help before starting a new project?

Absolutely. Many businesses begin with consulting to avoid costly mistakes, define priorities, and build a clear implementation strategy.

Is every consulting engagement customized?

Yes. Every business has unique goals, challenges, and requirements. DeTLeng provides recommendations tailored specifically to your organization.
