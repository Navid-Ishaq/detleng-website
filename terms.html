Successful Projects Require Collaboration

The most successful engagements are built on active collaboration between DeTLeng and the client.

DeTLeng Responsibilities

  • Provide technical expertise
  • Recommend practical solutions
  • Maintain professional communication
  • Deliver agreed project work
  • Document implementation processes
  • Support solution validation

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide project requirements
  • Supply relevant data and information
  • Grant required access where applicable
  • Review deliverables promptly
  • Provide timely feedback
  • Support validation and acceptance

Project Scope & Commercial Agreements

Every engagement is scoped individually based on business objectives, data availability, technical complexity, and expected deliverables.

Projects may be structured as:

  • Fixed Price Projects
  • Milestone-Based Projects
  • Hourly Consulting
  • Retainer Engagements

Commercial terms, timelines, deliverables, and project assumptions are agreed before work begins.

Where requirements evolve significantly during delivery, scope, timelines, and pricing may be reviewed collaboratively.

Professional Expectations

Technology projects involve technical, operational, and organizational variables that extend beyond software, databases, and reporting tools.

While DeTLeng applies professional standards, industry best practices, and practical implementation methodologies, business outcomes depend on factors such as:

  • Data Quality
  • Business Processes
  • User Adoption
  • Operational Execution
  • Organizational Readiness
  • Strategic Decision-Making

For this reason, project success should be evaluated through collaboration, implementation quality, and long-term business value rather than isolated technical outputs.
