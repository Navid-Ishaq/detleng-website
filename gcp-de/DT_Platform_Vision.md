# DeTLeng Platform Vision

## Platform Entry Point

**URL**

```text
https://platform.detleng.com
```

The platform becomes the single entry point for customers. Users log in once and access every data engineering capability from one unified workspace.

## User Experience

When a user clicks **Airflow**, they remain inside the **DeTLeng Platform**.

Airflow works as the orchestration engine behind the scenes, while the customer experiences a single, consistent DeTLeng interface.

The goal is not to expose infrastructure.

The goal is to provide a complete **Data Engineering Platform**.

---

## Long-Term Vision

```
DeTLeng Platform

        │

        ├── dbt
        ├── Python Jobs
        ├── Monitoring
```

Users should not need to remember separate URLs such as:

- airflow.detleng.com
- bigquery.detleng.com
- dbt.detleng.com

Everything should be accessible from one platform with one login.

---

## Design Philosophy

> **One Platform. One Login. One Data Engineering Ecosystem.**

Built on modern open-source technologies including Apache Airflow, Google BigQuery, dbt, Python, and AI-assisted automation.

---

## DeTLeng Principle

Clients don't buy Airflow.

Clients don't buy BigQuery.

Clients don't buy dbt.

Clients buy a reliable platform that transforms raw data into trusted business decisions.

That platform is **DeTLeng Platform**.
