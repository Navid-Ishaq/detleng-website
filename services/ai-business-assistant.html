Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Business Assistant?

An AI Business Assistant is an intelligent software solution that helps automate repetitive tasks, answer questions, support employees, and improve business productivity using Artificial Intelligence.

Can the AI Assistant learn about my business?

Yes. DeTLeng develops AI Business Assistants that can work with your business knowledge, documentation, procedures, and approved information to provide relevant responses.

Can the AI Assistant support customers?

Absolutely. AI Business Assistants can answer common customer questions, provide product information, guide users through processes, and improve customer response times.

Can the assistant integrate with business systems?

Where appropriate, DeTLeng can develop AI solutions that work alongside your existing business applications, databases, and workflows to provide a more connected experience.

Will AI replace my employees?

No. The goal is to support your team by automating repetitive work, reducing manual effort, and allowing employees to focus on higher-value responsibilities.

Is every AI Assistant customized?

Yes. Every organization has unique business processes and objectives. DeTLeng develops AI Business Assistants specifically tailored to your operational needs.
