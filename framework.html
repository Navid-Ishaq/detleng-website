How We Work

A Structured Approach to Delivering Reliable Data Solutions

At DeTLeng, we believe successful data projects begin with understanding the business behind the data.

Our goal is not simply to build dashboards, reports, or pipelines.

Our goal is to deliver practical solutions that improve reporting, increase operational visibility, and support better business decisions.

Every project follows a structured process designed to ensure quality, accuracy, and long-term value.

Step 1: Understanding Your Business Requirements

Every engagement starts with a discussion about your business objectives, reporting challenges, and data environment.

We work to understand:

  • Business goals

  • Existing reporting processes

  • Data sources

  • Operational challenges

  • Key performance indicators (KPIs)

  • Desired outcomes

This helps ensure the solution is aligned with your business needs from the beginning.

Step 2: Data Assessment

Once requirements are understood, we assess the available data and systems.

This may include:

  • Data quality evaluation

  • Source system review

  • Spreadsheet analysis

  • Database assessment

  • Reporting process evaluation

The objective is to identify opportunities for improvement and establish a reliable foundation for reporting and analytics.

Step 3: Solution Design

Based on the findings, we design a solution tailored to your requirements.

Depending on the project, this may involve:

  • ETL & ELT Pipelines

  • Data Transformation Workflows

  • BigQuery Data Warehouses

  • Power BI Dashboards

  • Reporting Automation

  • KPI Monitoring Frameworks

  • Business Intelligence Solutions

Every solution is designed with scalability, maintainability, and business usability in mind.

Step 4: Development and Implementation

Once approved, development begins.

Project deliverables may include:

  • Automated Data Pipelines

  • Data Models

  • BigQuery Implementations

  • Power BI Reports

  • Executive Dashboards

  • KPI Reporting Systems

  • Data Quality Improvements

  • Business Reporting Solutions

All deliverables are tested and validated before final delivery.

Step 5: Delivery and Business Insights

After implementation, solutions are delivered along with relevant documentation and recommendations.

Where appropriate, we also provide insights and observations that help organizations better understand their data and reporting environment.

Our objective is not only to deliver a solution, but to help clients gain greater visibility into their business performance.
