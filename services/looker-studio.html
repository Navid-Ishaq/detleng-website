Frequently Asked Questions

What is Looker Studio?

Looker Studio is Google's reporting and data visualization platform that transforms business data into interactive dashboards and shareable reports.

Why should my business use Looker Studio?

Looker Studio makes business reporting simple, interactive, and accessible by presenting data through clear visualizations that support better decision-making.

Can DeTLeng connect Looker Studio to our existing data?

Yes. DeTLeng connects Looker Studio with BigQuery, Google Sheets, SQL databases, cloud applications, APIs, and many other business data sources.

Can reports update automatically?

Absolutely. DeTLeng can configure automated data refresh so your dashboards always display the latest available information.

Is Looker Studio suitable for executive reporting?

Yes. Looker Studio is ideal for executive dashboards, operational reporting, marketing analytics, sales performance, and business KPI monitoring.

Can every dashboard be customized?

Yes. Every organization has unique reporting requirements. DeTLeng designs Looker Studio dashboards specifically for your business goals and operational needs.
