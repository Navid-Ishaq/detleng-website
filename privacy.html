DeTLeng Privacy Policy

Trust Begins with Reliable Data

At DeTLeng, we believe that successful data projects are built on trust.

Organizations share business information, reporting requirements, operational processes, technical environments, and strategic objectives with the expectation that their information will be handled responsibly and professionally.

We take that responsibility seriously.

This Privacy Policy explains how DeTLeng collects, uses, protects, and manages information shared through our website, consultations, and professional engagements.

Why Data Privacy Matters

Every Data Engineering and Analytics project involves business information.

Whether the source is spreadsheets, databases, cloud platforms, reporting systems, operational applications, or analytical environments, organizations need confidence that their information is handled with professionalism and care.

At DeTLeng, privacy is not simply a legal requirement.

It is a professional responsibility.

Our Privacy Commitment

Our commitment is simple:

Your Data. Your Business. Your Ownership.

DeTLeng does not sell client information, share confidential business data, or claim ownership of any information provided during consultations or project delivery.

Information shared with DeTLeng remains the property of the client or its rightful owner at all times.

Information We May Collect

To evaluate requirements, communicate effectively, and deliver services, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Company Information
  • Email Address
  • Contact Details
  • Project Requirements
  • Business Objectives
  • Reporting Challenges
  • Technical Requirements
  • Website Usage Information

Information is collected only when necessary to respond to inquiries, provide services, improve communication, or support project delivery.

Information Shared During Consultations

Organizations may contact DeTLeng regarding data engineering, analytics, reporting automation, data warehousing, ETL development, business intelligence, and related consulting services.

To evaluate potential solutions, clients may voluntarily share:

  • Business Requirements
  • Operational Challenges
  • Reporting Needs
  • Sample Datasets
  • Technical Documentation
  • Process Descriptions
  • Existing Reports
  • Analytics Requirements
  • System Architecture Information

All information is treated with professional confidentiality and used solely for consultation, project evaluation, communication, and agreed service delivery.

Responsible Access Management

Certain projects may require temporary access to systems, databases, cloud platforms, reporting environments, or analytical tools.

Any access provided is used exclusively for agreed project work, testing, implementation, support, or maintenance activities.

Access rights remain under the control of the client and may be modified or revoked at any time.

At DeTLeng, access is treated as a responsibility, not a privilege.

Your Data. Your Ownership.

A core principle at DeTLeng is clear:

Your Data Always Remains Yours

DeTLeng does not claim ownership of:

  • Client Data
  • Databases
  • Reports
  • Dashboards
  • Documentation
  • Configurations
  • Business Processes
  • Project Requirements
  • Intellectual Property
  • Proprietary Information

Ownership remains with the client at all times.

Confidentiality Is a Professional Obligation

Business information often represents years of operational knowledge, investment, expertise, and strategic effort.

For that reason, DeTLeng treats project discussions, datasets, documentation, technical environments, reporting requirements, and business information with professional confidentiality.

Information shared during consultations or project engagements is not disclosed to third parties unless:

  • Required by applicable law
  • Authorized by the client
  • Necessary for approved project delivery

Professional integrity and confidentiality are fundamental elements of every engagement.

Responsible Information Handling

Protecting business information is a priority throughout the project lifecycle.

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help:

  • Protect Communications
  • Safeguard Credentials
  • Reduce Unauthorized Access Risks
  • Support Secure Project Delivery
  • Maintain Information Integrity
  • Promote Responsible Data Practices

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, DeTLeng is committed to handling information responsibly, professionally, and in accordance with accepted business practices.
