Frequently Asked Questions

What is Power BI?

Power BI is Microsoft's Business Intelligence platform that transforms business data into interactive dashboards, reports, and visual analytics.

Why should my business use Power BI?

Power BI makes it easier to understand business performance through interactive visualizations, helping teams identify trends, monitor KPIs, and make data-driven decisions.

Can DeTLeng connect Power BI to our existing systems?

Yes. DeTLeng integrates Power BI with SQL databases, BigQuery, Excel, ERP systems, CRM platforms, cloud applications, APIs, and many other business data sources.

Can dashboards update automatically?

Absolutely. DeTLeng can connect Power BI to automated data pipelines so dashboards always display the latest available business information.

Will Power BI work with our Data Warehouse?

Yes. Power BI integrates seamlessly with modern Data Warehouses such as BigQuery and other database platforms, providing fast and reliable reporting.

Is every Power BI dashboard customized?

Yes. Every organization has different KPIs, business processes, and reporting requirements. DeTLeng designs dashboards specifically around your business needs.
