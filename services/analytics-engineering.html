Why Analytics Engineering Matters

Even with a modern Data Warehouse, reporting problems can still occur when business logic is inconsistent or data is not properly organized.

Common challenges include:

  • Different departments reporting different KPI values
  • Complex SQL queries for every report
  • Duplicate business calculations
  • Difficult-to-maintain reporting models
  • Confusing datasets
  • Slow dashboard development
  • Inconsistent business metrics

DeTLeng solves these challenges by creating standardized analytics models that provide a single, trusted version of your business data for reporting and decision-making.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Analytics Engineering?

Analytics Engineering is the process of organizing, modeling, and preparing business data so it can be used efficiently for reporting, dashboards, and Business Intelligence.

How is Analytics Engineering different from Data Engineering?

Data Engineering focuses on collecting, integrating, and storing data. Analytics Engineering focuses on preparing that data for analysis by creating business-friendly models, standardized KPIs, and analytics-ready datasets.

Why is Analytics Engineering important?

Without proper analytics models, reports often contain inconsistent calculations, duplicate logic, and unreliable KPIs. Analytics Engineering ensures every report is based on trusted business data.

Can DeTLeng work with our existing Data Warehouse?

Yes. DeTLeng can build analytics models on top of your existing Data Warehouse, BigQuery environment, SQL databases, or cloud data platform.

Does Analytics Engineering improve dashboard performance?

Yes. Well-designed analytics models simplify reporting, reduce unnecessary calculations, and improve the performance of dashboards and Business Intelligence tools.

Can Analytics Engineering support Power BI and Looker Studio?

Absolutely. DeTLeng prepares analytics-ready datasets that integrate seamlessly with Power BI, Looker Studio, and other Business Intelligence platforms.
