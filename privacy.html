DeTLeng Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Business Information

At DeTLeng, trust is the foundation of every client relationship.

When organizations share business information, reports, datasets, requirements, or operational challenges with us, they expect professionalism, confidentiality, and responsible handling of information.

We take that responsibility seriously.

This Privacy Policy explains how DeTLeng collects, uses, protects, and manages information shared through our website and consulting engagements.

Our Privacy Commitment

Our commitment is simple:

Your Data. Your Business. Your Ownership.

DeTLeng does not sell client information, share confidential business data, or claim ownership of any information provided during project discussions or service delivery.

All information shared with DeTLeng remains the property of the client or its rightful owner.

Information We May Collect

To evaluate project requirements and communicate effectively, we may collect information such as:

  • Name

  • Company Name

  • Email Address

  • Contact Information

  • Project Requirements

  • Business Objectives

  • Reporting Challenges

  • Technical Requirements

  • Website Analytics Information

Information is collected only when necessary to respond to inquiries, provide services, improve communication, or support project delivery.

Information Shared During Consultations

Organizations may contact DeTLeng regarding:

  • Data Engineering Projects

  • ETL & ELT Development

  • Power BI Dashboards

  • BigQuery Solutions

  • Reporting Automation

  • Business Intelligence

  • Data Quality Assessments

  • Analytics Engineering

To evaluate potential solutions, clients may voluntarily share:

  • Business Requirements

  • Reporting Needs

  • Technical Documentation

  • Process Descriptions

  • Sample Data

  • Project Specifications

  • Existing Reports and Dashboards

  • System Architecture Information

All information is treated with professional confidentiality and used solely for project evaluation, communication, and service delivery.

Client Systems, Credentials & Access

Certain projects may require DeTLeng to access client systems, cloud platforms, databases, analytics environments, APIs, or reporting tools.

Examples may include:

  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP)

  • BigQuery

  • Power BI

  • SQL Databases

  • Cloud Storage Services

  • Data Warehouses

  • Analytics Platforms

  • Business Intelligence Tools

  • Third-Party APIs

Clients may choose to provide:

  • API Keys

  • Service Accounts

  • OAuth Permissions

  • Database Credentials

  • Cloud Access Permissions

  • Reporting Platform Access

  • Technical Configuration Information

Such access is used exclusively for the delivery, testing, support, and maintenance of agreed project services.

DeTLeng does not sell, transfer, disclose, or use client credentials for any purpose beyond authorized project work.

Clients retain ownership and control of their systems, accounts, permissions, and access rights at all times.

Access may be revoked by the client upon project completion or at any other time deemed appropriate by the client.

Your Data Always Remains Yours

DeTLeng does not claim ownership of:

  • Client Data

Confidentiality & Professional Standards

Business information is often one of an organization's most valuable assets.

DeTLeng treats all client communications, project discussions, datasets, reports, credentials, system configurations, technical documentation, and business requirements with professional discretion.

Information shared during consultations or project engagements is not disclosed to third parties unless:

  • Required by applicable law

  • Authorized by the client

  • Necessary for approved project delivery

We follow a business-first approach based on:

  • Professional Integrity

  • Confidentiality

  • Responsible Data Handling

  • Access Control Awareness

  • Respect for Client Ownership

  • Information Security Best Practices

Protecting client information is a fundamental part of our consulting standards.

Information Security

Protecting business information is a priority.

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to help:

  • Protect Communications

  • Safeguard Client Credentials

  • Reduce Unauthorized Access Risks

  • Maintain System Integrity

  • Protect Project-Related Information

  • Support Secure Service Delivery

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, DeTLeng is committed to handling information responsibly, professionally, and in accordance with accepted business practices.
